HISTORY OF PRESENT ILLNESS
----------------------------------------------------
Mr. Marcus Webb, a 42-year-old male, presented to the Springfield Regional Medical
Center Emergency Department on March 14, 2024, following a motor vehicle accident
earlier that afternoon. Patient reported being stopped at a red light at the
intersection of Oak Ridge Blvd and 5th Ave, Springfield, when his vehicle was struck
from behind by another vehicle. He denies loss of consciousness. He reports immediate
onset of cervical and lumbar pain, with associated muscle spasm and limited range
of motion noted at initial evaluation.

Patient has no prior history of cervical or lumbar injury. He is employed full-time
as a logistics coordinator and reports that symptoms have interfered with his ability
to perform sustained sitting and lifting required for his occupation.
